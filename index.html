YASUO

The Unforgiven



The top lane in League of Legends is full of fighters, divers, and beefy tanks just waiting to kill new players, but for those that love a good old 1 vs 1 brawl, the role can offer some of the most intense experiences in the entire game.

AKALI

The Rogue Assassin



Jungle is arguably the most unique role in League of Legends as instead of heading to one of the three lanes, the Jungler clears neutral monsters in the jungle and looks to influence different areas of the map.

LUX

The Lady of Lumiosity



Mid Lane has always been a crucial role in League of Legends, as from its central position Mid Laners are able to influence the entire map if they want, or simply scale and be a major carry later on.

JINX

The Loose Cannon



The Bot Laner is usually an Attack Damage Carry, more commonly known as an ADC. These marksmen often scale extremely well into the game when they have bought enough items but are often weak early and are vulnerable to assassination and burst at all stages of the game.

THRESH

The Chain Warden



In the laning phase, the Support accompanies a carry in bot lane and helps them farm, stay safe and ideally get some kills too. Supports do not take farm for themselves and thus have a limited income, which their support item helps with.

LEONA

The Radiant Dawn



While able to engage enemies in combat, a tank's purpose isn't usually to kill opponents; rather, tanks excel at disrupting enemies and diverting focus to themselves, allowing them to lock down specific targets, as well as remove (or peel) threats from their allies.
